Privacy Policy

Delivery Date Estimator & Countdown Timer
Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

Delivery Date Estimator collects minimal data necessary to operate the app:

Store information: Your store name, domain, and timezone are used to configure the delivery date calculations for your storefront.

App settings: Your configured shipping speeds, cutoff times, holidays, and widget styling preferences are stored to display the widget on your product pages.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Customer personal information (names, emails, addresses)
- Payment or financial data
- Order details or purchase history
- Browsing behavior or analytics
- Cookies on your customers' browsers

How We Use Data

Store and settings data is used solely to calculate and display estimated delivery dates on your product pages. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

Data Storage

App configuration data is stored securely on our servers. If you uninstall the app, all your data is deleted within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

The delivery date widget runs entirely in the customer's browser using JavaScript. No customer data is sent to our servers or any third-party service. All calculations happen client-side.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR requirements. Since we do not collect customer personal data, there is no customer data to request, export, or delete. For store owner data deletion requests, uninstalling the app will remove all stored settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
